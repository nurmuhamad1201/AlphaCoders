    Rent a car from Avis or Budget – and get extra Miles and discounts!

    Rent a car

    Explore new routes while enjoying Miles&Smiles car rental privileges. Miles&Smiles members can get thousands of extra Miles and 35% discount when renting a car from Avis or Budget. For a more enjoyable journey with your loved ones, you can review the most suitable car rental options of Avis and Budget according to the space and comfort you will need during your journey.

    Miles to be earned with Avis auto rentals

    With Miles&Smiles privileges, you can earn thousands of extra Miles by renting an automobile from Avis, depending on the rental duration. Avis auto rental services are available at 84 locations in Türkiye and approximately 5,500 locations in 165 countries.

    Advantages with Budget vehicle rentals

    With Miles&Smiles privileges, you can earn thousands of extra Miles for auto rentals from Budget, depending on the vehicle group and number of days, and benefit from a 35% discount for rentals within Türkiye. Budget auto rental services are available at 5,462 locations in 134 countries and 57 locations in Türkiye.

    1500 Miles for economy group rental vehicles

    You can earn a total of 1,500 Miles for vehicle rentals of 3 or more days in the economy group.


    2500 Miles for luxury group rental vehicles

    Regardless of the duration, you can earn a total of 2,500 Miles on vehicle rentals in the luxury group.


    35% discount on domestic rental cars

    You can benefit from a 35% discount on vehicle rentals within Türkiye.

    Frequently asked questions about automobile rentals

    1. How old do I have to be to rent a car? Learn more

    You must be over 21 years of age to rent a car.

    2. Which documents are required to rent a car? Learn more

    • Driver's license
    • Credit card (must contain the renter's name, surname and card number)
    • Photo ID for international rentals (e.g. passport or driving license)

    3. How do I earn Miles with auto rentals? Learn more

    When you book your auto rental, enter your Miles&Smiles account number in the relevant field. The individual renting the car and the Miles&Smiles member must be the same person.

    4. How are auto rental prices determined? Learn more

    Prices are determined according to criteria such as the selected vehicle model, the rental period, and the pick-up and drop-off locations.

    5. When will the Miles I’ve earned on auto rentals appear in my account? Learn more

    The Miles you earn are usually credited to your account within six weeks from the date the vehicle is returned.

    6. Where can I collect and return my vehicle? Learn more

    With Avis and Budget, you can pick up and drop off your vehicle from a location convenient to you. Avis operates in approximately 5,500 locations in 165 countries and Budget operates in 5,462 locations in 134 countries.
